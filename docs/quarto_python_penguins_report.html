Introduction

Penguins are a family (Spheniscidae) of aquatic flightless birds that live primarily in the Southern Hemisphere. Their diet consists of:

  • Krill
  • Fish
  • Squid
  • More fish

There are 18 species of penguin, including:

  1. Macaroni penguin (Eudyptes chrysolophus)
  2. Chinstrap penguin (Pygoscelis antarcticus)
  3. Gentoo penguin (Pygoscelis papua)
